Legacy Timeline

1958

Born to Perform

Michael Joseph Jackson born on August 29th in Gary, Indiana, the eighth of ten children in the Jackson family.

1964

The Jackson 5

Joins his brothers in The Jackson 5, showcasing extraordinary talent from age six.

1971

Solo Career Begins

Launches solo career with "Got to Be There" while still performing with The Jackson 5.

1979

Off The Wall

First adult solo album becomes a massive success, establishing him as a solo superstar.

1982

Thriller Revolution

Thriller becomes the best-selling album of all time, revolutionizing music videos and pop culture.

1987

Bad Era

Bad album debuts at #1, producing five consecutive #1 singles.

1992

Dangerous Tour

Dangerous album and world tour, continuing his reign as the King of Pop.

2009

This Is It

Announces final concert series "This Is It" in London. Passes away on June 25th, leaving an indelible legacy.
